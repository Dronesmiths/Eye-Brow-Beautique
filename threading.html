The Process

Why Choose Threading?

Threading is a centuries-old hair removal technique that originated in South Asia and the Middle East. Unlike waxing, which can tug on delicate facial skin, threading uses a simple twisted cotton thread to roll over and lift hair right from the follicle.


This allows for unparalleled precision, creating clean, sharp lines that define your natural bone structure. It is gentle enough for sensitive skin and compatible with those using retinols or chemical peels.
