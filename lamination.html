What is it?

A Perm for Your Brows

Brow Lamination is the latest innovation in brow artistry. It's often described as a "perm" for your eyebrows, but instead of curling them, it sets them in a uniform, vertical direction.


This process hides small gaps and gives the illusion of major hair growth. It's the perfect solution for messy, unruly brows or for anyone wanting that high-fashion, fluffy runway look without the daily gel maintenance.

Common Questions


Brow lamination typically lasts between 6 to 8 weeks. To maintain the health of your brow hair, we recommend waiting at least 8 weeks between treatments.

For the first 24 hours, you must keep your brows dry (no water, steam, or sweat) and avoid touching them. After that, we recommend applying a nourishing oil nightly to keep the hairs hydrated.
