Common Questions


Regular tint is designed to only dye the hair itself. Henna is a natural plant-based product that stains the skin for up to 2 weeks and the hair for up to 6 weeks, giving a fuller, filled-in appearance.

With Henna or Hybrid Tint, the skin stain typically lasts 7 to 14 days, depending on your skin type (oilier skin fades faster) and skincare routine. The hair color will last up to 6 weeks.
